Access Control Policy
Purpose:
Define access control requirements for the AegisSecure Cloud Platform (ASCP) consistent with NIST SP 800-171 Rev 2.

Scope:
Applies to all users, administrators, devices, and processes accessing ASCP.

Policy:
- Access must be restricted to authorized identities authenticated through Entra ID GCC High and Keycloak.
- Devices must be Intune-managed and meet compliance policies.
- Access permissions follow Least Privilege and Zero Trust principles.
- Reviewer roles may access CUI data; administrative roles cannot.
- Project enclave users may only consume metadata APIs.
- Privileged access requires Privileged Access Workstations (PAWs) and Just-In-Time approval.
- Quarterly access reviews must be performed by the ISSO.
- All access is logged and monitored by the SOC.

Enforcement:
Non-compliance may result in access revocation, incident reporting, and disciplinary action.
